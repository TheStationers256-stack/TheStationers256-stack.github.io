Privacy Policy

At The Stationers, we respect your words and your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your manuscripts, personal data, and digital footprint.

1. Introduction

The Stationers ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://thestationers.com and the associated mobile application "The Stationers Author App" (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our authors, visitors, and app users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data, in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Console Developer Policies.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to facilitate the publishing process and improve our services. This includes:

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, and postal address for contract generation and royalty payments.
  • Manuscript Data: Book drafts, cover art ideas, and metadata (blurbs, genre info) uploaded to our secure servers.
  • Financial Data: Bank account details or PayPal addresses processed securely via our payment partners (Stripe/PayPal) for royalties and service fees. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (UUID) when using our App.

3. Android & Google Play Requirements

For users of "The Stationers Author App"

A. Permissions & Sensitive Data

Our app may request access to specific device features. In accordance with Google Play policies:

  • Storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Strictly used to allow you to upload manuscript files (PDF/DOCX) from your device to our portal.
  • Camera/Photos: Used only if you choose to upload a profile picture or cover reference image.

We do not access your contacts, SMS, or location data at any time.

B. Data Safety & Encryption

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS 1.2+. We define our data safety practices in the Google Play Console, certifying that we do not sell your personal data to third-party data brokers.

C. Account Deletion (App)

As required by Google Play policy, users can request full account deletion directly within the App settings (Profile > Settings > Delete Account) or by visiting our web portal. Upon deletion request, all personal identification is removed within 30 days, though legal publishing contracts may require retention of specific transaction records for tax purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

Service Fulfillment

To edit, format, and distribute your book to retailers like Amazon and IngramSpark.

Communication

To send publishing updates, royalty statements, and contract revisions.

Legal Compliance

To file copyright registrations and assign ISBNs in your name.

App Functionality

To sync your project status across devices and authenticate your login.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with necessary partners to fulfill our service:

  • Retailers: Book metadata is sent to Amazon KDP, Apple Books, and Barnes & Noble for listing purposes.
  • Printers: Manuscript files are sent to print-on-demand facilities (e.g., IngramSpark) solely for printing orders.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics and Firebase (for the App) to monitor system performance. These tools collect anonymized usage data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Regardless of your location, we extend these rights to all authors:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct incomplete or inaccurate data via your dashboard.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request we delete your data, subject to tax/legal retention laws regarding published works.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a parent discovers that a child under 13 has provided us with data, please contact us immediately for deletion.
